About us

“We are supporters of the spread of the Kazakh language. In our endeavor, we see the importance of preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage through the use of our native language. Our mission is to inspire people to appreciate the uniqueness of the Kazakh speech, preserve its authenticity and maintain communication in the language that is the key to our history and identity."
